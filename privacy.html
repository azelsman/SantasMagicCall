Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Santa’s Magic Call (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your family’s privacy and is committed to making this experience warm, safe, and magical. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

  • Parent information you provide. When you reserve a Santa call, we collect your email address so we can deliver your private call link and follow-up videos.
  • Child information we do NOT collect. We do not ask for your child’s name, age, location, or any identifying details. Santa interacts through imagination only.
  • Voice call transcripts. The call audio is not recorded, but a text transcript is temporarily generated so Santa can respond in real time. Transcripts are automatically deleted after your session unless needed for troubleshooting.
  • Automatic device data. Basic technical information (browser type, device type, IP region) may be collected automatically to keep the service running securely.

2. How We Use Information

  • Deliver your Santa call and follow-up videos.
  • Support safe and age-appropriate interactions during the call.
  • Improve technical performance and protect against misuse.
  • Email you updates related to your purchase.
  • Send optional holiday content only if you opt in (you can unsubscribe anytime).

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share only what is necessary with trusted service providers who help us operate the experience, such as:

  • Payment processing
  • Email delivery
  • Cloud hosting and security
  • Voice/AI infrastructure that powers the Santa call

These partners may only use your data as instructed by us and must protect it. We may also share information if required by law or to prevent misuse.

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies to keep the website functional, remember preferences, and understand basic usage patterns. You can control cookies through your browser settings at any time.

5. Data Retention

We keep your email and order information as long as needed to provide the service. Santa call transcripts are automatically deleted shortly after your session unless required for troubleshooting.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access or delete. You may request a copy of your data or ask us to delete it.
  • Email preferences. You can unsubscribe from optional messages anytime.
  • California / EEA rights. You may have additional rights depending on your region.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal data. No system is 100% secure, but we take security seriously and continuously improve protections.

8. Children’s Privacy

Santa’s Magic Call is designed for families, but parent involvement is required. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or allow children to create accounts.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we’ll revise the “Last updated” date at the top of the page.
